## Brand aesthetic (per project brief + IG content)
The elements to carry into the new site:
- **Logo** — square lockup (profile mark is 1080×1080). Need the vector/source.
- **Seashells** — recurring motif; should become a structural design device (dividers, section markers, backgrounds), not clip-art.
- **Vintage pin-up-inspired illustration** — the distinctive identity layer; sultry-retro, mid-century. This is the differentiator vs. the current generic Squarespace site.
- **Color world (from current site):** warm cream `#EFECDC` + deep oxblood `#471515`. Fits the vintage direction; extend with a blush/coral and a metallic (gold/brass) for the pinup + shell world.

## Gap vs. current website
The Instagram is rich, art-directed, and on-brand. The current eatumbo.com is a **stock Squarespace template** with none of this identity (no logo/shells/pinup, broken alt text, tiny type, 2-item nav, "catering" framing). The entire brand equity lives on IG and needs to move onto the site.
