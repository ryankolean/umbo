Reservations

We do things the analog way

Reservations are by phone only — give us a ring and a real person picks up. No apps, no online booking: we’re new to the neighborhood and we’d genuinely rather talk to you. It’s a little room — 25 seats at the bar, no tables — so we take reservations for parties of two to eight. And we always hold at least a third of the bar for walk-ins, so if the mood strikes, just stop in and put your name down. Craving something to go? Give us a call and we’ll see what we can do.
