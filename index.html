What We Pour & Shuck

The bar program

Three things we care about more than we probably should.

01

Oysters

A rotating raw bar of East and West coast oysters, shucked to order with our mignonettes.

02

Tinned Fish & Conservas

A cult little pantry of sockeye, clams, mussels and angulas — the good stuff, with bread and butter.

03

Caviar

Siberian and Osetra by the gram, done the classic way — because some days call for it.

The Room

Vintage seaside, up north

Seashells, warm cream, and a wink of old-postcard glamour. Umbo is a small room built for long lunches — a raw bar, a few bottles, and the kind of afternoon that stretches.
